🌿 Rishi Vashishtha speaks to Rama and Lakshmana
“Rama, yesterday you learned to remain as awareness while action unfolds.
Today, you must refine this further.
It is not enough to remain aware…
👉 You must remain unmoved within awareness.”
🪔 The Deeper Distinction
“Listen carefully:
Awareness sees thoughts
The witness knows them
But still, a subtle disturbance may remain…
A faint ripple:
👉 ‘This should not be happening’
 👉 ‘This is unpleasant’
 👉 ‘This must change’”
Vashishtha pauses…
“This is subtle identification.”
🌊 The Hidden Attachment
“Rama, even when you say:
👉 ‘I am the witness’
If there is resistance to what appears…
You have not yet become स्थिर (fully steady).”
🔥 Today’s Teaching: Effortless Allowing
“Do not interfere with the play of mind.
Let thoughts arise
 Let emotions move
 Let actions unfold
But remain as:
👉 That which does not resist
 👉 That which does not cling”
🧘 Practice in the Midst of Life
When a thought arises
Do not push it away
 Do not follow it
👉 Simply see: ‘A thought is known’
When emotion arises
Do not control it
 Do not justify it
👉 Simply see: ‘A sensation is moving in awareness’
When action happens
Do not claim it
 Do not judge it
👉 Simply see: ‘Action is unfolding’
⚖️ The Subtle Shift
“Lakshmana, yesterday you stepped back from identification.
Today, go one step deeper:
👉 Do not even resist what is seen.”
🌿 The Sign of True Stability
“How will you know this is maturing?”
Vashishtha explains:
Thoughts may be fast or slow
Emotions may be strong or soft
But within you:
👉 No contraction
 👉 No inner argument
 👉 No disturbance
🕊️ The Still Center
“Rama, be like the sky.
 Clouds may gather, thunder may roar…
But the sky does not resist the storm.”
✨ Final Upadesh (Instruction)
“Do not try to purify the mind.
 Do not try to perfect experience.
Remain as the one in whom
 all imperfection appears and dissolves.
This is freedom amidst action.”
