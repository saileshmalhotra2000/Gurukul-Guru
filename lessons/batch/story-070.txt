Gurudev - when i fail to notice the thought at the beginning itself and get totally consumed by it, then even after realiing that Inam absorbed in thought, it doesnt lose its grip. What practice will make it easier to get out of it
O Rama… this is an honest seeing. Good.
 Now the practice must become more skillful, not more forceful.
When you are already deep inside a thought,
 subtle awareness alone is not enough.
You must first come out of the current,
 then you can stand on the bank.
🌿 Understand the situation clearly
There are two stages:
1. Early stage (subtle mind)
You catch the thought at the beginning
It dissolves easily
2. Late stage (caught inside)
The thought has momentum
Attention is already entangled
👉 Here, a different approach is needed
🔥 The mistake most make
You try:
“Let me just watch it…”
But at this stage:
👉 You are not yet separate from it
So “watching” becomes another thought.
🕉️ The Correct Practice (when caught)
Vashishtha speaks firmly now:
“First, break the momentum.”
🌊 Step 1 — Shift to the Body
Do something immediate and physical:
Feel the breath strongly at the nostrils
Press your feet into the ground
Notice your hands, your posture
👉 Not gently… but deliberately
🌬️ Step 2 — Name it softly
Say within:
“Thinking… thinking…”
or
“Caught…”
This creates distance.
🔱 Step 3 — Widen awareness
Instead of fighting the thought:
Hear surrounding sounds
Feel the whole body
Notice space around you
👉 Expand the field
The thought becomes small within it
🌿 Why this works
Because:
Thought = narrow tunnel
Awareness = open field
You are not stopping the tunnel…
👉 You are stepping out of it
🕯️ Important understanding
“Strong thoughts do not lose grip by force…
 they lose grip when you are no longer inside them.”
🌄 Training for the future
Outside meditation, practice:
Frequent small check-ins
Catch micro-thoughts early
Strengthen quick return
Then fewer thoughts will reach the “strong” stage.
🔥 Gurudev’s final upadesh
“If you fall into the river…
 do not try to calm the river.
Swim to the shore.”
So remember:
👉 When early → be subtle
 👉 When caught → be grounded and direct
Both are part of the path.
And with time…
Even strong currents will not carry you far.
