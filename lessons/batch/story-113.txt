12 June 2026 — Lesson: The Moment Importance Is Assigned
The morning sun filtered through the forest canopy.
Birdsong drifted across the river.
Rama and Lakshmana sat quietly before Vashishtha.
For many days they had been studying:
the first taste
the first spark
the gathering of attention
the formation of pathways
the hidden charge beneath repeated visits
Today Vashishtha placed several stones upon the ground.
Most were ordinary grey pebbles.
Among them he placed a small golden bead.
Then he asked Rama,
"Which one did your eyes notice first?"
"The golden one."
"And why?"
"Because it seemed important."
Vashishtha smiled.
"Did it announce its importance?"
"No, Master."
"Then where did the importance come from?"
Rama became silent.
The Teaching
Vashishtha picked up the golden bead.
"Today we study a subtle event."
He placed the bead among the stones again.
"An experience appears."
"A sound."
"A thought."
"A memory."
"A sensation."
Then he paused.
"Something extraordinary happens."
"Importance is assigned."
He drew a circle in the sand.
Inside it he wrote:
Experience
Around it he drew a larger circle.
Important
Then he looked at the brothers.
"The second circle does not come from the world."
"It is added."
Rama Asked
"Master, who adds it?"
Vashishtha replied,
"The charge."
The brothers listened carefully.
"The charge whispers:
'This matters.'
'Pay attention.'
'Do not let this go.'
'This concerns you.'
And attention obeys."
The Hidden Sequence
Vashishtha drew in the sand:
Experience
↓
Charge touches it
↓
Importance is assigned
↓
Attention gathers
↓
Center forms
↓
Story begins
"The world presents experience."
"The charge assigns importance."
"This distinction is rarely seen."
Ordinary Life Examples
During Conversation
Ten sentences are spoken.
Nine are forgotten.
One remains alive for hours.
Why?
Importance was assigned.
On the Golf Course
Many shots occur.
One poor shot continues returning.
The shot is gone.
The importance remains.
While Working
Dozens of emails arrive.
One sentence keeps returning.
The sentence is not pulling attention.
The assigned importance is.
During Meditation
Many thoughts arise.
One becomes fascinating.
One becomes troublesome.
One becomes special.
Importance has been added.
Experiential Insight
Close your eyes.
Allow experience to arise naturally.
A sound.
A sensation.
A thought.
Now observe carefully:
Can you detect the moment when something becomes more important than everything else?
Not because it truly is.
But because attention begins treating it that way.
This is one of the earliest visible movements of identification.
Today's Sadhana
Several times today, pause and ask:
What just became important?
Then ask:
Did the experience bring importance?
Or
Was importance assigned?
Stay with the question.
Do not answer intellectually.
Observe directly.
Final Teaching
Vashishtha said:
"Experience appears.
Charge assigns importance.
Importance gathers attention.
Attention forms a center.
The center creates a story.
See where importance is assigned,
and you discover where the story truly begins."
Rama asked,
"Gurudev, where does the charge reside?"
Vashishtha picked up the golden bead.
"Not in the bead."
He pointed to the river.
"Not in the river."
He pointed toward the forest.
"Not in the world."
Rama looked puzzled.
"Then where is it?"
Vashishtha touched his chest.
"Charge resides in the conditioned patterns of mind."
Then he touched Rama's chest.
"And it is felt in the body."
Two Sides of Charge
Vashishtha drew two circles.
In the first he wrote:
Memory
In the second:
Sensation
"The root of the charge lives in conditioning."
"The expression of the charge appears as energy in the body."
An Example
Two men hear the same criticism.
One laughs.
One suffers all day.
The words are identical.
The charge is different.
Therefore the attraction is different.
How Charge Appears
When charge is activated, it often appears as:
tightening in the chest
contraction in the stomach
pressure in the throat
restlessness
excitement
anticipation
heaviness
urgency
These are not the charge itself.
These are the body's expression of the charge.
The Deeper Location
Lakshmana asked,
"So is the charge in the body or in the mind?"
Vashishtha smiled.
"Neither answer is complete."
He pointed to a seed.
"The tree lives in the seed."
Then he pointed to a growing sapling.
"And yet the tree appears in the branches."
"The conditioning is the seed."
"The bodily energy is the branch."
"The repeated attention is the fruit."
For the Yogi
Most people study the fruit.
Some study the branch.
Few study the seed.
The yogi asks:
"What belief, fear, desire, attachment, or self-image is giving this experience its charge?"
There the root is found.
The Deepest Insight
Then Vashishtha became quiet.
After a long silence he said:
"Ultimately the charge does not reside in the world.
It does not reside in the object.
It resides in the relationship between conditioning and experience."
The golden bead itself is not important.
The mind makes it important.
The criticism itself is not painful.
The mind makes it painful.
The memory itself is not compelling.
The mind makes it compelling.
Final Teaching
"The world provides the experience.
Conditioning provides the charge.
The body reveals the charge.
Attention follows the charge.
The center grows around the charge.
Therefore, if you wish to understand the center,
do not study the object.
Study the charge."
So the conditioned patterns of the mind create a charge?
Vashishtha nodded.
"Yes, Rama.
That is very close."
Then he added:
"More precisely:
Conditioned patterns create the potential for charge.
When experience touches those patterns, the charge becomes active."
He drew a dry field in the sand.
Then he drew a spark.
"The field is conditioning."
"The spark is experience."
"The fire is charge."
Without the dry field, the spark dies.
Without the spark, the field remains quiet.
The fire appears only when the two meet.
Example
Suppose there is a self-image:
"I must be respected."
This is conditioning.
Someone criticizes you.
This is experience.
The moment they meet:
contraction appears
importance is assigned
attention gathers
The charge becomes active.
Another Example
Suppose that self-image does not exist.
The same criticism arrives.
Nothing much happens.
The experience touched no significant conditioning.
No charge appears.
Attention moves on.
A Refinement
Rama asked,
"So does conditioning create the charge?"
Vashishtha replied,
"Conditioning stores the charge."
"The experience activates it."
This is closer.
The Sequence We Are Discovering
Experience Appears
↓
Touches Conditioning
↓
Charge Activates
↓
Importance Is Assigned
↓
Attention Gathers
↓
Repeated Visits Occur
↓
Pathways Deepen
↓
Center Forms
↓
Story Appears
Vashishtha looked at Rama carefully.
"Most people believe:
The world upset me.
The yogi gradually discovers:
The world activated something already present."
This is a tremendous shift.
Because now the investigation turns inward.
Not toward blame.
Not toward suppression.
But toward understanding.
The Deepest Insight
Then Vashishtha erased the entire diagram.
Only Awareness remained.
And he said:
"Conditioning creates charge.
Charge attracts attention.
Attention creates the center.
But Awareness creates none of them.
Awareness merely illuminates the whole process."
The sky does not create the storm.
It simply reveals it.
Likewise, Awareness does not create the charge.
It reveals the charge.
And in that revelation, the charge slowly loses its power.
