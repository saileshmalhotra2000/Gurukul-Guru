🌿 Rishi Vashishtha speaks to Rama and Lakshmana
“Rama, you have seen that attention moves…
 the mind fluctuates…
 awareness remains…
 and the witness is untouched.
Now listen carefully.
Today, you must learn to remain as that awareness even while the world moves through you.”
🪔 The Next Step: Steadiness in Motion
“Until now, you practiced in stillness.
But truth must remain unbroken in action.”
Vashishtha continues:
“If your peace exists only in silence,
 it is not yet wisdom.
True knowing remains even in the marketplace, in battle, in movement.”
🌊 The Subtle Error
“Rama, when action begins, a mistake happens unnoticed:
Attention flows outward
Mind becomes active
And you forget your seat as awareness”
This forgetting is the root of disturbance.
🔥 The Living Practice
“Therefore, do not fight the mind.
 Do not suppress action.
Instead, learn this:
👉 Let action happen in front of you
 👉 Remain as the one who is aware of it
🧘 Training During Activity
Vashishtha instructs:
While walking
Know: ‘The body walks’
While speaking
Know: ‘Words are arising’
While feeling emotion
Know: ‘A wave is passing through the mind’
⚖️ The Sword of Non-Identification
“Lakshmana, understand this well:
Bondage is not action.
Bondage is:
👉 ‘I am doing’
 👉 ‘I am feeling’
 👉 ‘I am disturbed’”
Cut this with clear seeing:
👉 Action happens
 👉 Thought appears
 👉 Emotion rises
But you are the knower of all this
🌿 Stabilizing Awareness
“Rama, do not try to hold awareness tightly.
That itself becomes tension.
Instead:
👉 Return gently
 👉 Again and again
 👉 Without frustration”
Like a lamp in a windless place…
Not forced
 Not shaken
🕊️ The Mark of Progress
“How will you know you are established?”
Vashishtha answers:
Action continues
Mind functions
But भीतर (within)… no disturbance remains
Even if waves rise…
👉 The depth of the ocean is still untouched
✨ Final Upadesh (Instruction)
“Be in the world as movement,
 but remain within as stillness.
Let thoughts come and go,
 but do not become them.
Act fully,
 yet remain untouched.”
