Gurukul Lesson — The Fire of Reaction
The morning sun filters through the forest canopy.
 You sit before me, Rama… quieter now, more watchful than before.
Yesterday you learned to step out of thought.
 Today, you must see something deeper:
Not all thoughts bind you.
 Reaction is what binds you.
🔥 What is Reaction?
A thought arises — that is natural.
 But then:
You resist it
You cling to it
You judge it
You continue its story
👉 That movement… is reaction.
🌊 See this clearly
An event happens:
A memory appears
A fear arises
A desire pulls
In that very instant, there is pure arising.
But immediately after:
“This should not be happening”
 “Why am I like this?”
 “I must fix this”
👉 This is the second arrow.
🕯️ The Hidden Bondage
Listen carefully, Lakshmana:
“It is not the thought that traps you…
 it is your resistance or indulgence toward it.”
🌿 Today’s Practice — Non-Reaction
When a thought arises:
Do not:
Push it away
Follow it
Analyze it
Instead:
Let it be exactly as it is
Notice its presence
Do nothing to it
🌄 What will happen?
At first, the mind will protest:
“This is uncomfortable”
“Something must be done”
Let even this be.
🔱 The Deeper Seeing
Stay as the one who notices:
The thought
The urge to react
The discomfort
All of it… appearing.
🌬️ A subtle key
Do not try to be indifferent.
Be aware without interference.
There is a difference:
Indifference is suppression
Awareness is openness
🕉️ What you will discover
If you do not react:
👉 Thoughts complete their life on their own
 👉 Emotions rise and dissolve naturally
And slowly…
The mind loses its habit of pulling you in
🔥 Gurudev’s Upadesh
“The chain is not in the thought…
 it is in your response to it.”
Break the response—
And the chain falls by itself.
Sit under this tree today.
Watch closely.
Not the thought—
But your movement toward it.
That is where freedom is hidden.
