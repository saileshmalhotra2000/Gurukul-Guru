What If Peace Was Already Present?

Rama sat quietly near the river.
The forest had not changed.
The birds were still calling from the trees.
The water was still moving in its own rhythm.

Yet within him, a question had become soft enough to be seen.

Why does Harmony keep mattering to me?

Vashishtha looked at him gently.

Because it holds the promise.
The promise of Peace.

Lakshmana listened.

The sage continued.

But look carefully, Rama.
What if Peace was already present within you?
What if Harmony was not creating Peace, but only reminding you of what had never gone away?

The river flowed.
A leaf fell into the water and was carried away.

Rama watched the movement.

The leaf disturbed the surface.
But the river did not lose itself.

Today’s Sadhana
When Harmony feels important, pause and ask: What is this promising me? Then ask more quietly: What if that Peace is already here?

Final Teaching
Harmony is a reminder. Peace is not created by the reminder. Peace is discovered when attention returns to its source.
