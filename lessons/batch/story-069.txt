The Next Lesson: The Illusion of Doership (Kartā Bhāva)
You still feel, subtly:
“I am practicing”
 “I am observing”
 “I am progressing”
This “I” has become refined… but it is not yet gone.
🕉️ Observe Carefully
Sit beneath the silent trees.
Let an action happen—
 a breath, a movement, a thought.
Now look closely:
Did you create the thought?
Did you decide the next breath?
Did you construct the feeling?
Or did they simply… arise?
🔥 The Inquiry
When action happens, ask:
“Is there truly a doer here… or only happening?”
Do not answer quickly.
Watch.
🌊 What you will begin to see
Actions occur…
Thoughts arise
Words are spoken
The body moves
But the “doer”—
👉 is only a thought appearing after the action
Claiming:
“I did this”
🌿 Deeper Understanding
Just as wind moves the leaves,
 just as rivers flow without effort—
So too:
Life is moving… without a separate doer.
🕯️ Practice
Today, remain alert in simple acts:
Walking
Speaking
Eating
And gently notice:
Is there a controller… or only flow?
Do not force the answer.
Let it reveal itself.
🔱 The Subtle Shift
First, you saw:
👉 “I am not the thought”
Now you begin to see:
👉 “I am not the doer”
🌄 Gurudev’s final pointing
“When the doer dissolves,
 action remains… pure, effortless, and free.”
Walk this path with steadiness, O noble ones.
The knot is loosening.
