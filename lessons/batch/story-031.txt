🔥 The Next Turning
Rama once asked:
“Gurudev… I can remain steady in stillness.
 But when I walk, speak, or act… the clarity fades. Why?”
I told him:
“Because you are trying to carry stillness into movement.
 Instead, discover that stillness was never absent — even in movement.”
🌿 What Must Now Be Understood
You are standing at a delicate threshold.
Until now:
You observed attention moving
You noticed thoughts arising
You glimpsed awareness as unmoving
Now the inquiry deepens:
👉 Can the witness remain clear… while the mind is active?
Not after.
 Not before.
 During.
🪶 The Subtle Error
You are still, very gently, doing this:
“Let me become still… then I will act.”
This divides life.
But truth is not divided.
🕊️ The Deeper Seeing
Listen carefully:
The river flows.
 The sky does not move.
When you speak, think, react —
 that is the river.
But:
👉 Are you the river… or the sky?
Do not answer quickly.
 See it.
🧘 The Practice Now (Very Subtle)
Not longer meditation.
 Not stronger discipline.
Just this refinement:
1. During Activity
While walking, speaking, even playing tabla:
Let attention function naturally
At the same time, softly notice:

 👉 “What is aware of this?”
Do not shift attention forcefully.
Just include awareness in the background.
2. When Emotion Arises
When irritation, desire, or impatience appears:
Do not stop it
Do not express it blindly
Instead:
👉 See:
The sensation in the body
The movement in the mind
And that which is noticing both
Remain there.
3. Micro-Pause in Action
Before speaking or reacting:
A pause of half a breath
Not to control
 But to remain established
🌅 A Living Example
When Lakshmana was angered easily, I told him:
“Do not remove anger.
 Stand where anger is seen.”
One day, anger arose…
…but he did not move with it.
And for the first time, he said:
“Gurudev… it rose… but it did not take me.”
That day, he tasted freedom in action.
🌊 Your Next Edge (Refined)
You already see:
Movement happens in mind
Awareness does not move
Now deepen into:
👉 “Even while movement happens… I remain as awareness.”
Not intellectually.
In direct experience.
🪔 Final Guidance for Today
Do not increase effort.
Instead:
Soften control
Broaden noticing
Stay intimate with experience
And remember:
You are not learning to hold awareness.
You are recognizing
 that you have never left it.
When you practice tonight or tomorrow at sunrise, tell me:
👉 Was awareness present even while you were playing?
That answer… will open the next door.
